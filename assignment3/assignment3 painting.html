Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean Auguste Dominique

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted by French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique in 1806 and todays hangs in Louvre. It is the third of the three portrait of Riviere family.

Date: 1806
Method: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 60*70 cm
Home:Louvre Museum, Paris
